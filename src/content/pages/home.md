introText: |
  The Creative Arts Association of Lakewood Ranch (CAALR) is a not-for-profit organization committed to inspiring professional visual artists. We provide education, resources, and networking opportunities for artists working in a wide range of mediums including watercolor, pottery, jewelry, stained glass, fiber art, mixed media, and more.

  Founded in 2001 as the "Creativity Group" when a handful of artists began gathering in a local park, CAALR has grown to include approximately 20 working artist members and over 50 supporting members. Since 2002, we have hosted seasonal art shows that attract hundreds of guests from across the Sarasota and Manatee county region.
