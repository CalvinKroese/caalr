Wilma Kroese wanted to express herself artistically. She tried her hand at painting watercolors but became dissatisfied. About 10 years ago, a friend introduced her to a pottery class. The native of the Netherlands, who is now an East Manatee resident, found her niche.

Kroese, a professional seamstress, learned about the association through ArtCenter Manatee and participates in the arts association showcases. She has a small kiln in her Mill Creek home but prefers the kiln at ArtCenter Manatee because it achieves hotter temperatures. She creates different styles — functional stuff and decorative — including vases, bowls and other pottery.

*By Dale White, Herald-Tribune — March 14, 2017*
